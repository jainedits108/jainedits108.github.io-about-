I didn’t start with a plan — I started with a passion.

Jain_edits108 began as a simple idea: to bring Jain Dharma into the digital world in a way that actually resonates. No fluff. No over-explaining. Just raw, powerful stories, edits, and concepts that speak to the soul.

But behind the screen?

Just one person — learning, exploring, editing, researching, and designing out of love for the Shasan. I’m not a scholar or guru — I’m just someone who fell in love with Jainism and wanted others to experience its depth.

As the journey grew, so did the community. What started as an idea turned into a shared vision, connecting people who resonate with this message. Every post, every edit, every message is part of something larger than just one person’s effort — it’s a movement to preserve and share our rich Dharma.

This isn’t just a page. It’s a vault of effort, late nights, drafts that never made it, and messages that changed lives — including mine.
